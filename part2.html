Kars is the primary antagonist in Part 2 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, titled "Battle Tendency." He is the leader of the Pillar Men, an ancient race with incredible abilities. Kars is highly intelligent, cunning, and ruthless, with a strong desire for power and immortality. He seeks the Red Stone of Aja, a powerful artifact capable of enhancing the abilities of the Pillar Men and granting them immortality. Kars is portrayed as a formidable opponent, demonstrating incredible combat skills and mastery over his body's biology, allowing him to adapt and evolve rapidly during battles. He possesses the ability to manipulate his body on a cellular level, granting him various forms and abilities. Throughout Part 2, Kars engages in intense battles against the protagonist, Joseph Joestar, and his allies as they attempt to stop his plans for world domination. Despite his intelligence and power, Kars is ultimately defeated in a climactic showdown atop a volcano, where he is launched into space by Joseph's clever tactics. Kars is remembered as one of the most iconic and powerful villains in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, known for his cunning nature, advanced abilities, and memorable battle sequences.
