In Part 6 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, titled "Stone Ocean," Father Enrico Pucci serves as the main antagonist. He is a priest with a fervent devotion to Dio Brando's ideology and is driven by a desire to bring about a new world order in accordance with Dio's vision. Pucci is introduced as a chaplain at Green Dolphin Street Prison, where the story primarily unfolds. He befriends Jotaro Kujo's daughter, Jolyne Cujoh, while secretly manipulating events to further his own goals. Pucci's ultimate ambition is to achieve a state of "Heaven," which he believes can only be realized by accelerating time and resetting the universe. To do this, he seeks the power of the "Green Baby," a mysterious organism with ties to Dio's legacy. Throughout Part 6, Pucci employs cunning tactics and manipulative schemes to achieve his objectives, including manipulating memories and Stand abilities using his own Stand, "Whitesnake." He forms alliances with other Stand users and poses a significant threat to Jolyne and her companions as they attempt to thwart his plans. The conflict between Pucci and Jolyne's group escalates to a dramatic showdown as they race against time to prevent the cataclysmic consequences of Pucci's actions. The battle between Pucci and the protagonists culminates in a climactic confrontation with far-reaching consequences for the fate of the universe. Enrico Pucci is remembered as one of the most complex and compelling villains in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, characterized by his unwavering devotion to Dio's ideals, his strategic brilliance, and his pivotal role in the events of Part 6.
