Part 1: Dio Brando

Dio Brando is a central antagonist in Part 1 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, titled "Phantom Blood." He is introduced as an orphan taken in by the wealthy Joestar family, becoming the adopted brother of the protagonist, Jonathan Joestar. Dio is cunning, manipulative, and power-hungry, seeking to gain control over the Joestar fortune and destroy Jonathan's life. He utilizes the power of a mysterious stone mask to transform into a vampire, granting him immense strength and regeneration. Throughout Part 1, Dio's relentless pursuit of power leads to numerous conflicts with Jonathan and his allies. He is ultimately defeated in a climactic battle atop a burning ship, but his legacy and influence continue to reverberate throughout the series.
