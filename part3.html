In Part 3 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, titled "Stardust Crusaders," Dio Brando returns as the main antagonist. He is revived after being dormant for a century and seeks to take over the world using his newfound powers as a vampire. Dio's ultimate goal is to achieve "Heaven," a state of ultimate power and control. Throughout Part 3, Dio manipulates Stand users and sends assassins known as Stand users to eliminate the Joestar family's bloodline, specifically targeting Joseph Joestar's grandson, Jotaro Kujo, and his companions on their journey to defeat him. Dio's Stand, "The World," grants him the ability to stop time for a brief period, giving him a significant advantage in battles. Dio is portrayed as charismatic, arrogant, and manipulative, using his wit and cunning to outsmart his adversaries. He revels in his newfound powers and delights in causing suffering to those who oppose him. The conflict between Dio and the Stardust Crusaders culminates in a climactic battle in Cairo, Egypt, where Jotaro and his allies confront Dio in a final showdown. Despite Dio's formidable abilities, Jotaro ultimately emerges victorious, defeating Dio and putting an end to his reign of terror once and for all. Dio's character in Part 3 is remembered for his iconic presence, intense battles, and his memorable catchphrase "Muda muda muda!" which he utters during his fights.
